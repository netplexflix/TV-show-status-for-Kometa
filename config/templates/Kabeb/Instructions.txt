1. Download the Ratings overlay files (r0.png-R100.png) and place them in your kometa/config/overlays folder
2. Rename TSSK-config.yml to config.yml and save it in your TSSK/config folder. Enter your Sonarr API key.
3. Add "- url: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/netplexflix/Overlays/refs/heads/main/TSSK-Kabeb/TSSK-Kabeb.yml" to the overlay_files section of your TV library in your Kometa config
